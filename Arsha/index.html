About Us

We are Discover Solutions, your premier Salesforce and Cloud Consulting partner. We specialize in Salesforce implementation, support, and integration services to help maximize your investment in the Salesforce platform and other Cloud Technologies (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure). Our experts have over a decade of experience and knowledge to provide cloud solutions that can ensure you meet your business objectives.

Our team of experienced Salesforce consultants have a proven track record of successful Salesforce implementations, and are experts in implementing and customizing Salesforce to meet our clients' unique needs. With our expertise, we can help clients optimize their sales and marketing processes, improve collaboration and communication, and enhance overall business performance.

We are a high-functioning and cost-effective consulting team with experience from various public and private sectors, including finance, education, and health. Our Perpetual Flux work model and onshore/offshore staff places your business needs front and center around the clock.

Our ultimate goal is to ensure that our clients achieve the maximum value from their investment in Salesforce and cloud technologies. We measure our success by our clients' success, and we are committed to building long-term partnerships with our clients to ensure that we can continue to help them grow and succeed.
